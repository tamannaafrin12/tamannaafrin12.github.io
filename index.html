Research Profile

About Me

Experimental condensed-matter physicist with five years of hands-on experience in cryogenic quantum transport, van der Waals device fabrication, and materials characterization of topological magnetic systems.

My research centers on separating intrinsic device physics from extrinsic measurement artifacts through controlled temperature-, field-, and angle-dependent experiments on van der Waals magnetic heterostructures.

Author of peer-reviewed work in Nature Communications and Nano Letters, with additional publications in Materials Research Express and RSC Advances.
